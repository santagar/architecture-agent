# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Provide actionable and evidence-based feedback.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or intimidation.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors who violate this code.

## Reporting

Report incidents through the security/support channels described in `SECURITY.md` and `SUPPORT.md`.
